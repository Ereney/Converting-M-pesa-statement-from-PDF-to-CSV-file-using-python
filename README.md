## Converting M-pesa statement from PDF to CSV format
M-pesa is a mobile phone based money transfer, financing and microfinancing service. It is a Kenyan product that is mojorly used as a payment system. Being a payment system, then its statement (M-pesa statement) can be used for individual or company credit analysis.<br>
Safaricom is the company who owns M-pesa and they provide the M-pesa statement in pdf format only. For analysis purposes, it is always preferable to have your data in csv format and thus the need of converting it to csv.
### Steps 
-Step 1. Uploading the pdf file
-Step 2. Dropping the unnecessay rows, e.g the first 9 rows that contains the summary part of the statement
-Step 3. Saving the file as CSV
